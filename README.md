# 🏀 NBA 2025 Draft Quiz

A web app where you guess what pick each player was selected in the 2025 NBA Draft and earn points based on accuracy.

## How to Play
- You are shown a player's name and team
- Guess what pick they were selected in the 2025 NBA Draft (1-15)
- Points are awarded based on how close your guess is:
  - Exact: +10 points
  - Within 2: +7 points
  - Within 5: +4 points
  - More than 5 off: +0 points
- 10 rounds per game, max score is 100
